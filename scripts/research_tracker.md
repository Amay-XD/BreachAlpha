## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Duplicates**: Check if company already in dataset (different breach dates?)
2. **Date Format**: Always YYYY-MM-DD, discovery date not attack date
3. **Ticker Errors**: Verify actively traded, not delisted/merged without noting
4. **Missing Sources**: Need minimum 2 credible sources per breach
5. **Speculation**: Don't include "alleged" or "rumored" - only confirmed
6. **Out of Scope**: Exclude pre-2010, <100K records (unless high impact)
7. **Record Count**: Verify millions/thousands notation (147M = 147 million)
8. **Severity Mismatch**: High severity requires substantial impact or records
